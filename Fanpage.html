Band Biography

Chromeo is Dance/Electro-Funk band from Montreal, Canada. They formed in 2002 after meeting at Coll√®ge Stanislas in Montreal.

They released thier first album in 2004, titled "She's in Control." The album led to conparisons to 80's hit makers "Hall & Oates."

Their second release, "Fancy Footwork," released in 2007 and peaked at #11 on the US electronic charts and earned them world wide recognition as an up and coming electronic/funk duo.

Chromeo's third album, and first major label release, "Business Casual," recieved critical and commercial success. Peaking at #70 on the Billboard 200, and #4 on the US Dance/Electronic albums. The album featured the hit, "Night by Night," and the track, "Night by Night," which had additional vocals by pop star Solange Knowles.

"White Women," Chromeo's fourth sudio album, released in 2014, was thier first worldwide success. Reaching the top 25 in Australia, Canada and the US. The ablum featured a large array of guest appreances including Vampire Weekend's Ezra Koenig, Toro y Moi, Solange Knowles, LCD Soundsystem's Pat Mahoney, and Fool's Gold duo Oliver. One review called the album, "the best Chromeo record yet, a seamless combination of energy and melancholy, disco and soul, all mixed together into some of the most effective songs they've written."

Chromeo's fifth album, "Head over Heels," was released in 2018. It peaked at #1 on the Billboard US Dance/Electronic Albums chart. "Head Over Heels" features vocals from artists such as The-Dream, French Montana, Stefflon Don, DRAM and Amber Mark. Rolling Stone noted that it was "pop songs about dating mishaps, comically disastrous relationships and anxiety-inducing sexual encounters." Juice Music Video
